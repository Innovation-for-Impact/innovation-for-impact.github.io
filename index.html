Our Projects

The objective of Innovation for Impact is to enable a students to take up
industry-relevant skill training that will help them
in securing a better livelihood.

MTutor

MTutor is a mobile app designed to help University of Michigan students find reliable tutors on campus. The project focuses on intuitive app design and includes a specific feature aimed at enhancing user experience.


Inora Technologies

Inora Technologies is focused on developing and enhancing Organic Intelligence (OI) Core Technology, the first universal, numerically balanced platform that provides absolute structural determination. This project involves exploring various improvement options for the technology, applying databases, C programming, and web development to enhance its functionality and effectiveness.


K-9

K-9 is dedicated to enhancing the safety and well-being of our local community by providing professionally trained K9 units for security purposes. They offer a range of services that include deploying skilled dogs and handlers to assist in law enforcement efforts, event security, and neighborhood patrols.
