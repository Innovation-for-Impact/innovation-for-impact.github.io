FAQ

+

What services does Innovation for Impact provide?

Innovation for Impact specializes in web development, application creation, database optimization, and IT consulting. Our team of skilled members undertakes hands-on projects to deliver customized solutions that meet your company's unique needs, all while fostering continuous learning and skill development.

+

How does our pro bono consulting work?

We offer pro bono consulting services by partnering with organizations to take on their tech projects at no cost. This allows your company to benefit from our expertise while giving our members valuable hands-on experience. We handle everything from project planning and execution to deployment and maintenance, ensuring your project goals are met efficiently.

+

What expertise does your team have in handling technical projects?

Our team comprises passionate individuals with diverse backgrounds in computer science, software engineering, UI/UX design, and database management. Through continuous learning and practical experience gained from real-world projects, our members are well-equipped to tackle a wide range of technical challenges effective

+

Can you customize solutions to fit our specific business needs?

Absolutely! We understand that each business has unique requirements. Our approach is highly flexible, allowing us to tailor our services to align perfectly with your objectives. Whether it's integrating specific functionalities into your website or developing bespoke applications, we ensure our solutions support your business goals effectively.

+

What is the process to engage with Innovation for Impact?

1. Contact Us: Reach out through our website or email to discuss your project needs.
2. Project Assessment: We assess your requirements and propose a customized plan.
3. Collaboration Agreement: Once agreed, we outline the project scope, timeline, and deliverables.
4. Project Execution: Our team begins working on your project, maintaining regular communication.
5. Delivery & Support: We deliver the final product and offer ongoing support to ensure your satisfaction.
